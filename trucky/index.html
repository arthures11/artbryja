Trucky

Trucky is a comprehensive CRUD web application designed to automate and optimize the operations of logistics, forwarding, and transportation. It replaces traditional paper-based workflows and basic spreadsheet usage with a centralized, intelligent system to streamline daily tasks, enhance productivity, and improve competitiveness in the transport services market.

The system is tailored to meet the specific needs of transport company, focusing on process automation, legal compliance, and real-time collaboration. Trucky offers features that simplify employee and subcontractor management, scheduling, task allocation, real-time vehicle tracking, and communication between all stakeholders.

The platform emphasizes high performance, secure transaction handling, and a user-friendly experience. Key features include:

Centralized account management for company-employed drivers and independent subcontractors.

Real-time tracking and reporting of driver's work schedules, ensuring compliance with applicable legal regulations.

Tools for creating, approving, and analyzing driver schedules.

Assignment and reconciliation of individual tasks, ensuring accountability.

Real-time monitoring of vehicles to track their location and performance.

Efficient management of vehicle assignments to specific drivers and tasks.

Dedicated messaging features for seamless communication between everyone.

Automated analysis and reconciliation of driver's actual working hours.

Integration with legal compliance requirements for labor laws and operational standards.

Device-specific interfaces tailored for desktop and mobile use, ensuring accessibility and usability for all user groups.

A centralized notification system to coordinate actions between dispatchers, owners, and drivers.
