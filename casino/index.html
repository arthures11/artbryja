Casino

A web-based casino simulation platform developed as a university project, featuring three classic gambling games: Dice, Roulette, and Coinflip. Key features include:

  • •Dice:Players bet on over/under outcomes with customizable odds.
  • •Roulette:Traditional number-based betting system.
  • •Coinflip:Simple red/black betting game.
  • •User account management with balance tracking.
  • •Game history and statistics.
  • •Real-time user activity monitoring.
  • •Secure authentication system with social login support.
  • •Bonus system with periodic rewards.
  • •Multi-language chat system (English/German).
