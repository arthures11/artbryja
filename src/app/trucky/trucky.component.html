Trucky

Trucky is a comprehensive CRUD web application designed to automate and optimize the operations of logistics, forwarding, and transportation. It replaces traditional paper-based workflows and basic spreadsheet usage with a centralized, intelligent system to streamline daily tasks, enhance productivity, and improve competitiveness in the transport services market.

The system is tailored to meet the specific needs of transport company, focusing on process automation, legal compliance, and real-time collaboration. Trucky offers features that simplify employee and subcontractor management, scheduling, task allocation, real-time vehicle tracking, and communication between all stakeholders.
