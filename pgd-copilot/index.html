Copilot

Due to the internal structure, this description is intentionally concise, focusing on the core purpose and key features of the application while highlighting my involvement in its development.

As part of my work for Next Era Energy, I have contributed to the development of PGD Copilot, an internal AI-powered chat application tailored to support technicians working with solar and wind energy devices. This tool leverages advanced artificial intelligence to deliver real-time access to schematics, troubleshooting guides, and expert guidance, enabling technicians to resolve issues efficiently and effectively within the company’s renewable energy operations.

The application prioritizes secure access, seamless information retrieval, and a practical user experience. Key features include:

AI-Driven Assistance

processing to interpret technicians’ queries and provide accurate, context-specific responses.

Secure Authentication

robust login security and role-based access control.

Real-Time Information

connects to the company’s internal knowledge base to instantly fetch resources.

Intuitive Interface

designed for ease of use across various devices, including on-site operations.

Knowledge Integration

powered by a company-specific knowledge base, ensuring responses are relevant and precise.

many more...

My role in the project

In this project, I was mainly responsible for the front-end development using Angular, where I focused on building and maintaining the user interface. My responsibilities went beyond initial development - I have actively worked on implementing new features to meet user needs and improve the overall experience. Very often, I've collaborated closely with the design team and back-end team to ensure smooth integration between the front-end and back-end systems, troubleshooting any issues that arise from their interaction. I've also played a role in identifying and fixing bugs, not only within the front-end code but also those stemming from the back-end, taking a proactive approach to ensure the application remains reliable and performs efficiently.
